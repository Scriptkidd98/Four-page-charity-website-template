About Flominence Foundation

Flominence Foundation was founded in 2021, and began out of a need to help alleviate the issues of menstrual/period poverty among young girls and young adults in Nigeria, by giving them access to sanitary products. This indirectly suports girl's physical, mental and emotional well-being during the time of their periods.

Vision/Mission

To support girls in maintaining good menstrual health and hygeine.
To address the stigma and shame around menstrual periods.
To ensure no girl child misses school during their menstrual periods.
To address the stigma and shame around menstrual periods.
To alleviate the emotional and mental challenges that comes with having no access to sanitary products.
To support the pupil's learning by providing stationeries needed for their studies.
